Our story

From a Lincoln kitchen,
to your freezer.

Nüuri was born out of our restaurant in Lincoln, where we saw something simple: people wanted real, nourishing food — but without the wait, the fuss, or the guilt of a compromise.

Under our parent brand Naturawell, we've spent years championing whole ingredients and honest preparation. With Nüuri, we've taken the clean-eating principles of our restaurant kitchen and locked them into something anyone can enjoy at home, at work, or anywhere in between.

Every bowl is crafted from real açai, whole fruits, and nothing unnecessary. No preservatives, no hidden sugars, no shortcuts. Just premium ingredients, gently frozen at peak freshness — so when you're ready, they are too.

Today we're in independent shops, cafés, studios and bowling alleys across the UK. Tomorrow, we're heading for the supermarket aisle — bringing real frozen food to every British kitchen.

The ritual

From freezer to bowl,
in four simple moments.

1

Unpack

Pull a tub from your freezer. Peel back the lid — find your granola, dried fruit & wooden spoon tucked inside.

2

Warm

Microwave the açai base for just 20 seconds — just enough to soften, never enough to melt. Best slightly soft.

3

Top

Tear open the sachet. Scatter crunchy granola and sweet dried fruit across the creamy surface.

4

Enjoy

Take a breath. Grab the wooden spoon. Dig in — any time of day, any place you like.
